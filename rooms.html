FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your stay.

Check-in is from 3:00 PM and check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged subject to availability — contact us in advance and we will do our best to accommodate you.

We require a one-night deposit to secure your booking, which is deducted from your final bill at checkout. The deposit is fully refundable if you cancel at least 48 hours before your check-in date.

Cancellations made 48 hours or more before check-in receive a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours are non-refundable. For group bookings of 3 or more rooms, a 7-day notice is required.

Yes, we are pet-friendly. Small pets under 10 kg are welcome in all room types with prior notice. A small supplement applies per night — please let us know at the time of booking.

Yes, we offer private on-site parking for guests at no extra charge. Spaces are first-come, first-served. Please mention your vehicle at check-in so we can reserve a spot for you.

We are happy to note floor or view preferences on your booking. While we cannot guarantee specific rooms, we do our best to accommodate requests. Guests booking the Super Room are automatically placed on higher floors with city views.
